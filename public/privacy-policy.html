Privacy Policy

SDC Boost Browser Extension

Last Updated: November 25, 2024

1. Introduction

SDC Boost ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a browser extension designed to enhance your experience on SDC.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Data Collection and Storage

2.1 Local Storage Only

SDC Boost operates entirely locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's local storage API.

2.2 What We Store Locally

The extension stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Module Settings: Your preferences for enabling/disabling extension modules (Age Filter, Age Highlighter, Ad Blocker, Chat Scroll Fix, Chat Export, Navbar Boost Button)
  • Module Configuration: Custom settings for each module (e.g., age ranges, filter preferences)

2.3 What We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history or website data
  • Account credentials or passwords
  • Profile information from SDC.com
  • Chat messages or conversations
  • Any data to external servers
  • Analytics or tracking data

3. Permissions

3.1 Storage Permission

SDC Boost requires the "storage" permission to save your module preferences and settings locally in your browser. This permission allows the extension to:

  • Remember which modules you have enabled or disabled
  • Save your custom configuration settings
  • Preserve your preferences between browser sessions

All data stored using this permission remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

3.2 Content Script Access

The extension runs content scripts on SDC.com pages to provide its functionality. These scripts:

  • Only operate on SDC.com domains
  • Modify page content locally in your browser
  • Do not send any data to external servers
  • Do not access data from other websites

4. Third-Party Services

SDC Boost does not use any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data collection services. The extension operates independently and does not communicate with external servers.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage mechanisms, your data is protected by:

  • Your browser's security features
  • Your operating system's security measures
  • The fact that no data is transmitted over the internet

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all extension data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the extension (which removes all stored data)
  • Using your browser's settings to clear extension data
  • Resetting all modules to their default settings through the extension's options page

7. Children's Privacy

SDC Boost is designed for use on SDC.com, which has its own age restrictions and terms of service. Our extension does not knowingly collect information from children, and since we do not collect any personal information, this policy applies to all users regardless of age.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

9. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data. You can:

  • Access your stored settings through the extension's options page
  • Modify or delete your preferences at any time
  • Uninstall the extension to remove all stored data

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how SDC Boost handles your data, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing or your preferred method of communication.
